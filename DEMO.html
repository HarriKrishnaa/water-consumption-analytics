ℹ️ 3AM Leak Detection Logic (Lambda + Timestream)

Daily Lambda Function: Runs at 4:00 AM IST, queries Timestream for each flat's 2-4 AM consumption over the last 7 days. If average nighttime flow exceeds 50 liters, the flat is flagged as a likely leak candidate. Alerts are written to DynamoDB and emails sent via SES to both the flat owner and society manager.
